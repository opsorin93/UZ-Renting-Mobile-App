FAQ

Do I need to pay a deposit?

In order to rent a property you will have to pay a deposit in advance. The amount is usually the rent for 1 month of the respective property.

What is the minimum rent period?

The shortest period of time you can rent a property is 3 months

How long does it take to rent out a property?

Depending on the specifics of your property and its location, property management companies can generally rent a house within 30-60 days.

Can I book a viewing?

Yes. Please contact us using the infomation available in the contact section and we will arrange one ASAP.

What are the office working hours?

Our customer service is open from 9am to 5pm, Monday to Saturday.

What do I need in order to rent a property?

Firstly you will need to register on the app and contact us. We wil be asking for a copy of your passport and confirmation of enrolment letter. After receving this information it can take us up to 3 working days to verify them.
